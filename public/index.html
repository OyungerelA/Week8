How It Works

This is a language sharing platform. On the screen will be displayed a simple word or phrase that people learn when they are first learning a new language (i.e. hello, goodbye). You can write the prompted word in your language(s) and once you submit the translated word, it will be displayed real-time on the screen. People from all over the world can come together and share their languages with each other and help one another to learn phrases in different languages.
